ABOUT JUST STUDENTS

JUST students is a group devoted to making IMS a better place for all students. We want to make sure that students of all races, genders, and sexual orientation are welcomed and accepted at IMS. WE BELIEVE that Every student at IMS belongs at IMS, Students deserve to feel safe, valued, respected, and welcome, People of all races, genders, sexual identities, religions, nationalities, disability, etc. belong at IMS and It’s ok not to know something. If you want to join us, we meet **MEETING DAY** at **MEETING TIME**. We want to hear your ideas too!

What's going well at IMS:

  • Our curricula in LA and SS are getting to be more varied and more representative of different groups
  • intention of not being to strict on homework is definitely working
  • Students are starting to understand history
  • Teachers and students can connect via email and Schoology, and students can feel free to ask any questions when necessary
  • people are generally well-intentioned
  • Students are beginning to get more educated on what’s happening in society and what has happened in history

What's not going well at IMS:

  • Teachers don’t always know how to address issues that come up
  • We have few groups that are specifically for various identity groups
  • While we do learn about history, not everybody seems to have been able to fully connect the dots on how it’s led to events today.
  • We have few groups that are specifically for various identity groups (eg we have QUILTBAG but we don’t have a Black Student Union, Asian Student Union, Jewish Student Union, etc.)
  • People say that they are not being racist, sexist, etc. when they are
  • Not that many people are trying to change things in terms of racism, sexism etc.
